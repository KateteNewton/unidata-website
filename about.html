Our Story

Built by students,
for the future.

UniData began in a crowded library corner with a simple realization: student life is complex, but the tools to manage it shouldn't be. We started as a group of peers looking for a better way to track academic progress and share knowledge. Today, we're building the definitive learning ecosystem for students across the region.
